Daphne's Laruel
Myth of Apollo and Daphne.
Apollo is the God of Sun in Roman's myth, he fall in crazy love with young girl, Daphne. The Sun God started his pursure to Daphne. The young lady, however, doesn't catch on this crazy love. But she can never escape from the Sun God. Hopeless, she became a laruel to avoid enthusiasm of the Sun God.
Instrustion
You will have the laruel tree,Daphne planted in the vase. Drag the blockes and control the crash of nutritions(balls) in the vase. Every time the nutritions hit the edge of vase, the flower will bloom a bit. Bars will control the speed and way that nutrition moves.Once the flower blooms, click to listen to Daphne's story.
